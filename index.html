MISSION

KBI aims to cultivate students and innovators around the globe by providing a leading platform for advanced technology competitions through the International Data Science Olympiad and the International Blockchain Olympiad. Our mission is to drive global impact by fostering a culture of innovation through the practical application of science, technology, and design, connecting...
